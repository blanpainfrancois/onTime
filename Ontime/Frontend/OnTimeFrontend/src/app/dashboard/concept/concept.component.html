What is OnTime™?

Ontime™ is an app/dashboard that we have created that will hopefully in the future will be known and used all over the world. Our goal is very simple. We’ve all had some days, where we had a meeting, school, work, etc and that we’ve been late to one of these atleast once. Our goal is to make it an easy and streamlined experience to: A: Let your superior know you are going to be late, how long and why. B: To let a superior easily monitor his workers and the times they’ve been too late.
